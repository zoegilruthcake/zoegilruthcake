Old Fashioned Fruit Cake. 4 drops almond essence. Fold in raisins and pecans.

200 gms sugar powder. Fold in candied fruit. At 140C fan 160C.

2 tsp vanilla essence.

Refrigerate for a minimum of 24 hours and up to 3 days. Before making the cake bring the fruit to room temperature and drain reserving any liquid for basting. Place the entire cake setup in the oven very carefully and bake the cake for 2 hrs. Fill the prepared pans a little over half full.
